Go to MUMBLE or Discord and say you want to play. We usually start at 20, it depends when the people will get together.

The administrator adds players, the algorithm divides based on the ranking of players, divides into two teams, so that the squads are equal.

Firstly, we use the ELO algorithm, secondly, going into details, the ELO algorithm takes the responsibility factor, which in our league is set so that the points for wars are counted for how many frags you scored during the war, but the most important thing is whether your team won or not.

Example: 5v5 war, team A won 15 to 13 with team B, in team A the best player had 40 frags and the worst frags 5, there is a possibility that even though the player with 5 frags who won the war will receive minus points, from which this it depends? From the distribution of frags in the team, and from the individual ranking.

See previous point but the other way around.

The team you play for must win, you must score as many frags as possible.

In case you lose on the team that was the favorite and you scored the least frags in it.

Try it, and we guarantee that you can't, even the best player needs team support.

There are also positive aspects to the coronavirus, and it's basically one. Old friends reunited for the first lockdown in March 2020. Since then, we play almost every day, and from November 2020 we created the Spearhead Mix League. Come to us on Mumble, play, see for yourself that it's worth it.

First CUP 2on2 OBJ Real in MoH:SH MIX League
Rules:
  1. Register on Discord Channel #cup until 20:00, 19.03.2021 (Friday)
  2. Example register in #cup channel on discord is: R - Nickname1/Nickname2
  3. The draw of teams for the Cup live on twitch at 21:00 Friday 19.03.2021
  4. CUP based rules: DOUBLE ELIMINATION (Winner and Loser Brackets)
  5. Time play: 7 rounds
  6. Each round 4 minutes
  7. Players have 7 days from selected to play their war
  8. 5 maps to choose from: The Hunt, V2, The Bridge, Stlo, VSUK Abbey
  9. Teams before war will baning maps i.e: Team 1 - ban Stlo, Player 2 - ban VSUK Abbey, Team 1 - chose The Bridge, Team 2 - chose V2,
    so they play The Bridge & V2 in case of draw next map will be The Hunt.
  10. Servers available: 173.199.83.169:12203 & 173.199.83.172:12203 or Applepitje War servers (ask admin)
  11. Teams are required to send screenshots with the results from the war to the admin. Admin can make a force screenshots druing the game, player must to send the screenshots to admin as soon as possible after war (10 mintus max!)
  12. Disputes are settled by the league committee (-Illu$ioN-, KaPsEL, bAtOn).
