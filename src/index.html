Rules:
  1. Register on Discord until 20:00, 05.02.2021
  2. Draw pairs 20:00 - 21:00, 05.02.2021
  3. CUP based rules: DOUBLE ELIMINATION (Winner and Loser Brackets)
  4. Players have 3 days(72hours) from selected to play their war
  5. Time play: First to 5 rounds
  6. Each round 4 minutes
  7. Maps: The Hunt, V2, The Bridge, Stlo, VSUK Abbey - each player before war start baning maps i.e:
    Player 1 - ban Stlo, Player 2 - ban VSUK Abbey, Player 1 - chose The Bridge, Player 2 - chose V2,
    so they play The Bridge & V2 in case of draw next map will be The Hunt.
  8. Disputes are settled by the league committee (-Illu$ioN-, KaPsEL, bAtOn).
